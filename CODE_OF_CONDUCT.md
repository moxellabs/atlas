# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Atlas welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Protect private information, credentials, proprietary docs, and security reports.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private communications or personal information without permission.
- Sharing credentials, tokens, private hostnames, proprietary docs, or vulnerability details in public issues.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior harms the community or project safety. Security issues should follow `SECURITY.md`.
